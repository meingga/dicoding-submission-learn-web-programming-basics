Periodic Servicing

Includes regular oil and other fluid changes in the vehicle. Each car already has recommendations that have been selected. Oil, oil filter, air filter, brake fluid, engine coolant, spark plugs, etc.



General Checkup

This is a thorough check of the car. Generally done when someone wants to buy a used car and ensure the car is in good condition. What is meant is of course to find out existing deficiencies or damage.



Tune Up

Generally tune ups coincide with regular service. According to the language, it means resetting, which is more to ensure that several engine circulation components remain in top condition. For example batteries, fan belts, spark plugs, combustion systems, etc.



Body Repair

Identical to painting a damaged or dented car body. Body panels that are heavily damaged must of course be replaced and finished with painting to match the color.



Diagnostic Scan

Using a computer connected to the car's brain to detect various anomalies. Cars are now increasingly sophisticated and have more and more sensors that can read various indications of damage. Can read malfunctions in the engine or transmission and various other electrical components.



Genuine Parts

Provide necessary spare parts for damaged vehicles. Basically every component on a car can be damaged and need replacement. You don't always have to provide original spare parts, because there are also many aftermarked spare parts which are more popular because they are more affordable.



Service AC

Driving comfort is of course supported by cold AC. Adding freon, even replacing spare parts, is necessary if the AC is no longer cold.



Modification

Not always workshops only deal with repairs. Modifications are provided for those who want to change the appearance of their vehicle to make it more beautiful. Rims, body kits, audio, window film, exhaust, etc.
